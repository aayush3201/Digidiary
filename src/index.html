Hello! This is DigiDiary - your very own virtual diary. You can now organize your routines, thoughts, actions, etc. all in one place, without having to flip through seemingly countless pages.

Note: This application stores your data on your browser. Which means that you cannot access your diary from another device or even another browser. However, this also ensures that no one except those who have access to your device have access to your diary!
